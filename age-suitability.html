Age Suitability

12+

Bible Study Features

The app is suitable for users aged 12 and over who want to study the Bible. All Bible texts, commentary, Strong's Concordance, cross-references, notes, highlights, and bookmarks are available without restriction.

16+

AI Study Assistant

The AI-powered Bible study assistant is restricted to users aged 16 and over. Users under 16 can use all other features of the app but will not have access to the AI chat functionality.

About the App

J&J Study Bible is a free Bible study app designed for personal scripture study. It includes multiple Bible translations (KJV, WEB, Louis Segond), Strong's Concordance with original Hebrew and Greek definitions, Matthew Henry's Commentary, and over 432,000 cross-reference pairs.

AI Safety Measures

The AI study assistant includes multiple layers of content safety:

  • The assistant is restricted to Bible-related topics only
  • All AI processing runs locally on the device — no data is sent to external servers
  • Content filtering ensures age-appropriate, scripture-focused responses
  • Off-topic or inappropriate requests are declined by the assistant
  • The AI age restriction (16+) provides an additional safeguard for younger users

Parental Guidance

While the app is designed to be safe and educational, we recommend that parents and guardians:

  • Review the app's features with their child before use
  • Encourage direct scripture reading alongside any AI-assisted study
  • Use iOS Screen Time or parental controls to manage app usage as needed

Common Questions

How do I download an AI model?
Go to the AI Chat tab, select a model from the picker, and tap Download. The app will download the model file over Wi-Fi. Models range from 400 MB to 2 GB depending on quality level.
Can I use the app offline?
Yes. All Bible texts, commentary, and concordance data are included in the app. The AI assistant also works offline once a model is downloaded. The only feature requiring internet is the initial model download.
How do I cancel a subscription?
Donations are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device, or visit Manage Subscriptions.
How do I delete my data?
All data (notes, bookmarks, highlights, chat history) is stored locally on your device. You can clear chat history from within the app, or delete the app entirely to remove all data.
The AI gave an inaccurate answer.
The AI study assistant is a tool to help with Bible study, not a replacement for direct scripture reading. Always verify AI responses against the actual Bible text. If you encounter persistent issues, please report them to us.
